Chicken Shawarma Bowl

Description

This middle eastern inspired Chicken shawarma recipe is easy to make from home, it's healthy and packed with awesome flavor. From fresh veggies to the savory flavor of the chicken, you'll be sure to find this recipe one of your favorites!

Ingredients

  • 5 boneless skinless chicken thighs
  • 2 lemons , juiced
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 6 cloves garlic , minced
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
